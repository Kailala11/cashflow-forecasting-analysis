##  Methodology

### 1. Data Collection & Preparation
- **Historical Data**: 3 months of actual financial performance (Jul-Sep 2024)
- **Revenue Streams**: 6 categories tracked
  - Food Sales (Rp 180-267M/month)
  - Beverage Sales (Rp 280-405M/month)
  - Snacks/Pastry (Rp 85-128M/month)
  - Delivery/Online (Rp 45-80M/month)
  - Event/Catering (Rp 15-40M/month)
  - Merchandise (Rp 8-16M/month)
  
- **Cost Centers**: 10 major expense categories tracked

### 2. Scenario Development
Three scenarios built with different growth assumptions:

| Scenario | Monthly Growth | Key Assumptions |
|----------|----------------|-----------------|
| Optimistic | 8-12% | Viral marketing success, increased foot traffic |
| Base Case | 4-6% | Steady operations, normal market conditions |
| Pessimistic | 0-3% | High competition, reduced mall traffic |

### 3. Statistical Analysis
- **Descriptive Statistics**: Mean, median, standard deviation, quartiles
- **Volatility Analysis**: Standard deviation of cash flows
- **Correlation Analysis**: Relationship between inflows and outflows
- **Trend Analysis**: Linear regression on revenue and expense trends

### 4. Break-Even Analysis
- Cost-Volume-Profit (CVP) modeling
- Fixed vs. Variable cost separation
- Sensitivity analysis on different fixed cost levels
- Safety margin calculation

### 5. Risk Assessment
- Scenario spread analysis
- Uncertainty quantification
- Downside risk evaluation
- Contingency planning
